Welcome.

I am a PhD student at the Université Côte D'Azur, where I am working on the design of a tool to generate a 3D model of the respiratory system from patient Ct-Scan.

This work is supervised by Benjamin Mauroy, researcher at the J.A. Dieudonné Laboratory (CNRS, UCA), and Angelos Mantzaflaris, researcher at Inria (Aromath team).

Topic of interest.

Deep Learning, segmentation

Methods for extracting important information from medical images, such as CT scans.

Geometric modeling, computer aided geometric design

Numerical simulation

Numerical simulations on mesh, using finite element methods.
